Story angles

What makes this worth telling

01

The druid who uses AI to protect the land from AI

A senior technology professional with an Oxford AI qualification who is also a practising druid. The tension between those two identities became the platform. Using the technology to fight the damage the technology is doing.

02

The fastest land grab in human history

AI infrastructure is being built at a speed that outstrips public scrutiny. Data centres on ancient land. Water tables being drawn down. Communities given 12 weeks to object to decisions that will last a century.

03

Built in seven hours — and what that means

A fully functional global platform, built alone in one night using AI. The story of what is now possible — and why the tools that are changing everything can also be used to protect everything.

04

Faith communities and the AI crisis

Every major spiritual tradition carries teachings about stewardship of the earth. The Firewalkers is bringing those communities — druids, Muslims, Christians, Buddhists, indigenous traditions — into a conversation that technology has failed to have.

05

Giving communities the tools to fight back

The platform gives ordinary people — no technical knowledge needed — AI tools to analyse planning applications, draft objection letters and connect with others in the same fight. Power redistributed through the very technology that threatens them.

06

The awen and the algorithm

A druidic concept of divine inspiration — the awen — arriving at midnight and producing a technology platform by morning. The intersection of ancient spiritual practice and modern AI. A story that hasn't been told before.

About the founder

Scott Seivwright

Scott Seivwright Founder, The Firewalkers · AI Strategist · Practising Druid

Scott Seivwright is a digital transformation and sustainability leader with thirty years of experience advising organisations on AI strategy, governance and technology change at board level. He holds the Oxford Artificial Intelligence Programme certification and has worked across global corporations, financial services, healthcare and the public sector.

He is also a practising druid — an Ovate student with OBOD, member of the Elder and Gorse Druids in Roslin and Awen Alba in Edinburgh. His druidic path connects him to the land, the sacred landscapes of Britain and Ireland, and an understanding of stewardship that predates modern environmentalism by millennia.

Scott co-founded GreenPO.org, works with the Green Software Foundation and Agilists for the Planet, and has spent years at the intersection of technology and sustainability. The Firewalkers is where those worlds finally came together.
