Forms — What They Do & Where They Go

Every form on the site, what it collects, and where the submission goes

Mailing list

Join the Movement — Mailing list signup

Found on: Homepage · Community

SendFox

Signs you up to The Firewalkers mailing list. You receive research releases, campaign alerts, and movement updates. Sent when there is something worth saying — never for its own sake.

Fields collected
First name Email address
Service SendFox mailing list
Data goes to SendFox mailing list. First name used for personalisation in emails as {{ subscriber.first_name }}. No data shared with third parties.
Partnership

Affiliate / Organisation Enquiry

Found on: Community

Web3Forms

For organisations — campaign groups, NGOs, community groups, academic institutions — who want to affiliate with The Firewalkers, share resources, or have their campaigns amplified to our network.

Fields collected
Organisation name Organisation website Contact name Area of work About your organisation Active campaigns to share GDPR consent checkbox
Service Web3Forms · subject: "Affiliate Application — The Firewalkers"
Data goes to Email to hello@firewalkers.earth. Reviewed personally by Scott. No automated processing.
Intelligence

Report a Data Centre Planning Application

Found on: Data Centre Watch

Web3Forms

For residents, journalists, councillors, and campaigners who have spotted a data centre application — or a threat — that should be on our radar. Submissions are reviewed and, where verified, added to the Data Centre Watch intelligence hub.

Fields collected
Location / site name Planning reference number Local planning authority Type of concern Consultation deadline Your name Email address Additional detail / links
Service Web3Forms · subject: "Data Centre Watch Report — The Firewalkers"
Data goes to Email to hello@firewalkers.earth. Verified submissions added to the Data Centre Watch page. Your contact details not published without permission.
Product feedback

Request a Tool

Found on: Tools

Web3Forms

Tells us what planning or campaigning tool would help you most. The most common and specific requests directly shape what gets built next. If you are facing a real planning situation, describe it — that is the most useful input we can receive.

Fields collected
Your name Email address Tool request description
Service Web3Forms · subject: "Tool Request — The Firewalkers"
Data goes to Email to hello@firewalkers.earth. Used to prioritise tool development. Email address only used to notify you when the tool launches, if provided.
