Start a Campaign

Something happening near you?

The most powerful campaigns are local. A community that knows a place, loves a place, and is willing to fight for it is more credible than any campaign consultant. Here is how to begin.

1

Name the issue clearly

What is happening, where, and who is behind it? A clear, specific statement is the foundation of every successful campaign. Use AI to help you research and articulate it.

2

Understand the process

Is this a planning application? A policy consultation? A procurement decision? Each has different deadlines and different pressure points. Know the rules before you play.

3

Build your evidence

Data, precedents, policy references, expert voices. AI can help you find and synthesise evidence that would have taken weeks to assemble by hand.

4

Gather your community

Who else cares about this place? Who has standing to object? A coordinated community response is exponentially more powerful than individual letters.

5

Bring it to the hearth

Share your campaign here. Other Firewalkers may have faced the same issue in a different place and can share what they learned.
