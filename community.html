Who is already here

Work with us

Is your organisation fighting for the same land?

The Firewalkers is building a directory of organisations — environmental groups, community campaigns, sustainability networks, faith communities — who are working on the same problem from different angles.

If you affiliate with us, we will list your organisation on this site, signpost our community to your work, and share your campaigns through our channels. You do the same for us. No fees. No bureaucracy. Just mutual amplification.

Affiliation is open to any organisation that is working to hold the technology industry accountable for its environmental impact, or to protect land and communities from infrastructure that moves faster than regulation.

What affiliation means in practice

  • Your organisation listed on the Firewalkers community page with a description and link
  • Your active campaigns signposted to our community via WhatsApp and the mailing list
  • Your resources shared in the Data Centre Watch intelligence hub where relevant
  • A reciprocal link or mention from your channels to ours
  • Nothing more than that unless you want it to be
