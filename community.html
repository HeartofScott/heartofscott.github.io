§ 02 — Allied OrganisationsAffiliates & partners

Organisations walking
the same fire

These organisations are aligned with the Firewalkers mission. They can help with legal advice, planning expertise, campaign support, and local knowledge. If your organisation wants to be listed, apply below.

Scotland — Planning Law

Planning Aid Scotland

Free, independent planning advice for communities and individuals across Scotland. Specialises in helping people understand and engage with the planning system, including objecting to major applications.

Free planning advice & community support
pas.org.uk
UK — Environmental Law

ClientEarth

Environmental lawyers using the law to tackle the climate and nature crises. Has successfully challenged planning decisions on environmental grounds across the UK and Europe.

Legal challenge & policy advocacy
clientearth.org
UK — Planning & Nature

CPRE — The Countryside Charity

Campaigns for the protection of rural England from inappropriate development. Has extensive experience challenging data centre and infrastructure applications in rural and Green Belt locations.

Campaign support & planning objections
cpre.org.uk
Ireland — Environment

An Taisce

Ireland’s national trust and leading environmental advocacy organisation. Has been at the forefront of challenging data centre developments on energy and environmental grounds across Ireland.

Environmental challenge & policy
antaisce.org
USA — Water & Energy

Food & Water Watch

Led the 230+ organisation coalition calling for a US moratorium on new data centre construction. Focuses on water and energy impacts of AI infrastructure with extensive research and campaign resources.

Research, coalition & US campaigns
foodandwaterwatch.org
