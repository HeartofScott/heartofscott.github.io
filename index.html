Find Your Grove

Druid & Earth-Spirit Organisations in Scotland

The Order of Bards, Ovates & Druids

International · Strong Scotland presence

One of the world's largest druid orders, with groves and study groups across Scotland. Offers a correspondence course through the three grades of bard, ovate, and druid.

druidry.org →

The British Druid Order

UK-wide · Bardic & Nature Focus

Emphasises nature spirituality, bardic arts, and working with the spirits of place. Active community with Scottish members.

druidorder.demon.co.uk →

The Druid Network

UK · Open & Inclusive

A charitable organisation supporting druids of all traditions, instrumental in gaining official recognition for druidry as a religion in the UK.

druidnetwork.org →

Ár nDraíocht Féin (ADF)

Indo-European · Reconstructionist

A reconstructionist druid organisation with a rigorous study programme rooted in scholarship of ancient Celtic and Indo-European traditions.

adf.org →

The Pagan Federation Scotland

Scotland · Pan-Pagan

The Scottish district of the Pagan Federation — organises moots, events, and Pagan Pride. Welcomes druids alongside witches, heathens, and all pagan paths.

paganfederation.org →

Clan na Gàidheal

Scotland · Gaelic Spirituality

Dedicated to the living Gaelic spiritual tradition — language, land, ancestral connection, and the old ways of the Gàidhealtachd. Deeply rooted in Scottish soil.

clannagaidheal.co.uk →

Caledonia Druid Network

Scotland · Informal Network

An informal network of druids and druid-friendly practitioners based in Scotland, sharing events, resources, and community across the country.

Find them on Facebook →

Historic Environment Scotland

Scotland · Sacred Sites

Custodians of Scotland's sacred sites — Callanish, Skara Brae, the Clava Cairns, and hundreds more. Essential for land-based practice.

historicenvironment.scot →

Hand-Picked by DHoS

DHoS Finds These Links Useful

These are sites, podcasts, writers, and resources I keep coming back to — things that have genuinely fed my path. Not a comprehensive directory, just what I actually use and love. I'll keep adding as I find more worth sharing.

🎙 Podcast

Druidcast

The official OBOD podcast — interviews, music, and talks from the druid community worldwide. One of the longest-running pagan podcasts out there. A great companion on long walks.

druidcast.libsyn.com 🎙 Podcast · Mythology · Scotland

House of Legends — Daniel Allison

Scottish storyteller Daniel Allison tells Celtic legends, Norse myths, and Irish folk tales with real warmth and depth. A beautiful voice for the old stories — the Salmon of Wisdom, Ossian, and more.

houseoflegends.me ✍️ Writer · Podcast · Mythology

Sharon Blackie — The Art of Enchantment

Scottish author of If Women Rose Rooted and Hagitude. Her Substack and podcast explore Celtic myth, fairy tales, and the feminine in the landscape. Deeply nourishing work.

sharonblackie.substack.com 🌐 Website

OBOD — The Druid Way

Beyond the organisation itself, the OBOD website is a rich resource — articles on druid philosophy, the Wheel of the Year, tree lore, and walking this path in the modern world.

druidry.org 🌐 Website · Scotland

Historic Environment Scotland — Sacred Sites

The definitive guide to Scotland's ancient sites — standing stones, cairns, brochs, and hillforts. Use it to plan pilgrimages. Callanish, Skara Brae, Clava Cairns and hundreds more.

historicenvironment.scot 🌐 Website · Scotland

Canmore — Scotland's Sites & Monuments

The national record of the historic environment of Scotland. Search any area of the country and find every recorded ancient site and place of significance. An extraordinary resource.

canmore.org.uk 🌐 Website · Mythology

Irish Myths

Scholarly but accessible deep-dives into Celtic mythology — the gods, heroes, and the Otherworld. Covers Irish and Scottish myth with care and real academic rigour.

irishmyths.com 🌿 Nature · Scotland

Trees for Life

Rewilding the Caledonian Forest in the Scottish Highlands. Rooted in the same reverence for ancient woodland that runs through druid practice. Volunteer, donate, or simply be inspired.

treesforlife.org.uk 📖 Texts · Mythology

Sacred Texts — Celtic Archive

Free access to a vast archive of Celtic texts and folklore collections — including the Carmina Gadelica, Alexander Carmichael's treasury of Gaelic prayers and incantations collected in Scotland.

sacred-texts.com/neu/celt 🏔 Scotland · Nature

Walk Highlands

The best guide to walking in Scotland — every hill, glen, and coastal path mapped and described. Essential for planning pilgrimages to sacred landscapes. The land awaits; this gets you there.

walkhighlands.co.uk 🏔 Scotland · Language

Gaelic Place Names of Scotland

Unlock the meaning hidden in the landscape. Every hill, loch, and glen has a Gaelic name that tells a story — this site helps you read the land in its original tongue.

gaelicplacenames.org 🏔 Scotland · Sacred Land

National Trust for Scotland

Protects some of Scotland's most sacred natural and historic places — Ben Lomond, Glencoe, Staffa and Fingal's Cave. Their properties are pilgrimage destinations for the earth-minded.

nts.org.uk 🌐 Website

The Druid Network

A generous resource hub for druids of all traditions — essays, book recommendations, and a community of practitioners. Good place to find others walking similar paths.

druidnetwork.org 🌐 Website · Mythology

Imbas — Celtic Reconstructionism

A deep resource for those drawn to reconstructing pre-Christian Celtic spiritual practice from the historical record. Rigorous scholarship in service of living tradition.

imbas.org 🎙 Podcast · Mythology

The Triskele Podcast

Three druids gather to share their paths — warm, honest conversations about what it actually looks and feels like to practice druidry week to week. Beginners and experienced practitioners alike.

Available on Apple & Spotify

How to Keep This Page Updated

1

Add a New Event

In community.html, find <div id="events-list"> and copy an existing event card. Update the date, title, location, description, and tags. Commit and it goes live automatically.

2

Add a Link to "DHoS Finds Useful"

Find the links-grid div and copy a link card. The data-cat controls which filter shows it. Use any of: podcast website writer scotland mythology nature

<a href="URL" target="_blank" class="link-card" data-cat="podcast scotland">
3

Event Submissions Come to Your Email

When someone fills in the submit form, it opens their email app with all details pre-filled to Heartofscott@gmail.com. Review it, then add it as a new event card (step 1).

4

Add Social Links When Ready

When you create your social accounts, just ask me and I'll build you a proper social section to drop straight into this page.
